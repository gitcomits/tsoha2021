Our teachers

Our dedicated teachers are a major factor in the success of the School of Online Studies . Teachers are drawn to the School of Online Studies because of our robust model that emphasizes project-based learning, art and technology and a supportive environment in which everyone is accountable for helping students reach the goal of attending university.

Our alumni

Our alumni are all those who have studied at School of Online Studies and its predecessor universities. Our network already includes almost 141,000 alumni in more than 80 countries. We are here to help you discover new insights, shape a meaningful career path, and learn life-wide.

Our Career Design Lab is a learning and collaboration space for shaping sustainable careers. We support School of Online Studies students and alumni in meaningful career design and lifewide learning. For employers, we offer opportunities for collaborating and connecting with School of Online Studies talent. We also serve School of Online Studies and all lifewide learners by sharing research-based insights and inspiring dialogue on the sustainable and inclusive future of careers.
